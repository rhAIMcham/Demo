The decision-maker The focus is on the behavioural process of the decision-maker, including psychological forces to select a preferred action and the propensity to risk avoidance or risk acceptance.
The organisation The locus of where the decision is made, the pursuit of organisational purposes that provide a foundation for assessing the decision. This aspect implies the reciprocal relationship between the organisation and management decisions.
The external environment Organisations and managers are part of the more extensive environment system. The external environment includes stakeholders with a vested interest in the outcomes of the managerial decisions.
The outcome The results that management expects when the decision is made. The mindset of the managerial decision-makers determines whether they will attempt to maximise the outcome of a given choice.
